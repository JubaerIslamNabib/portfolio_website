AIRGRIP - PNEUMATIC SOFT GRIPPER

Pneumatic soft robots, characterized by their lightweight design, high efficiency, and environmental adaptability hold a key position in this domain. We made a gripper with the intention of mimicking our hand finger movements. As soon as the finger starts to bent , the resistance value begins to increase which can be found due to input of the flex sensor. The motor starts when this resistance passes the threshold. Thus the fingers of the gripper bend and hold smaller weight .

Eco-Stride : A Prosthesis Made in Bangladesh for Below-Knee Amputees

Limb amputation in Bangladesh, driven by diabetes, accidents, and infections, affects many, especially in poor communities with limited access to affordable prosthetics. Our project aimed to create a below-knee prosthesis using locally available materials. We succeeded, with a total cost of just 7500 BDT, making it the cheapest option on the market. The prosthesis is eco-friendly, utilizing sustainable materials. After conducting mechanical tests and a clinical trial, our patient reported high satisfaction. The patient is walking again after 8 years.
year-2025

Modelling Drug Particle Transport and Deposition in the Tracheal Airway

We simulated inhaled drug delivery using a combination of Computational fluid dynamics (CFD) and physical airway models. This allowed us to replicate airflow and particle deposition patterns,particle trajectories, providing insights of drug dispersion in the respiratory system.
